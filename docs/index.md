# Sanguine

Sanguine is a minimal iOS app for logging periodic readings and tracking doses that may influence them over time.

## What it does

- **Record readings** — log a numeric value with a date, time, and optional note
- **Plan doses** — schedule upcoming doses day by day and mark them as administered
- **Track trends** — visualise readings and doses over time with charts and summary statistics
- **Stay on track** — optional reminders for readings and doses
- **Home screen widget** — shows your latest reading at a glance
- **Import & export** — bring in historical data via CSV, export at any time

## Privacy

All data is stored on your device. Nothing is sent anywhere.
