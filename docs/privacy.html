Privacy Policy

Sanguine — Last updated: March 2026

Sanguine is a personal logging tool for tracking periodic readings and associated doses. Your privacy is important. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Sanguine does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no analytics, no advertising, and no network connections of any kind.

Data Storage

All data you enter — readings, dose entries, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device. Data is kept in your device's app container and is included in iCloud backups if you have iCloud backup enabled for your device.

Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with third parties. The only way data leaves your device is if you explicitly use the Export function, which saves a CSV file that you can share as you choose.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses Apple's local notification system. No data is sent to any server to deliver these notifications.

Children

Sanguine is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

Questions can be submitted via the App Store review or support link.
